# 🪨 Speed Over Perfection

Ship fast. Fix later. The cost of being wrong is almost always lower than the cost of being slow.

This doesn't mean ship garbage — it means ship the smallest thing that tests the hypothesis. Jordan said it best: "The market doesn't care about your code quality. It cares about whether you solve their problem."
